How it runs

From vector to doorstep — four passes of the bit.

  1. 1

    Stage the file.

    Upload product photos, set price, write the maker's note, choose the edition size. Save as draft until the chips settle.

  2. 2

    Open the shop.

    Publish and your storefront updates live. Stripe Checkout handles payment; sales tax is calculated for the buyer's address.

  3. 3

    Cut, finish, sign.

    Order lands in your queue with material, dimensions, and a printable shop ticket. Mark stages as you go: cut, sand, oil, signed.

  4. 4

    Box it. Ship it.

    Print labels at live rates, send the customer a tracking note with a photo of the finished piece on the bench. Done.
