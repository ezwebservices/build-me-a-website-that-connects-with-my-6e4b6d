# Ironlace Studio

**Tagline:** Precision cut. Artfully made.

## Pitch
Ironlace Studio is a CNC garage shop where raw steel, walnut, and aluminum are coaxed into heirloom-grade objects. Every piece begins as a vector and ends as something you want to touch — signage, furniture inlays, wall art, custom hardware, and bespoke shop commissions. We sell direct from the garage floor, ship nationwide, and take custom orders for collectors, designers, and makers who want one-of-one work.

## Audience
- Interior designers sourcing statement pieces for clients
- Homeowners furnishing a remodel with bespoke metal/wood accents
- Small businesses needing signage with soul
- Fellow makers commissioning custom CNC work they can't cut themselves

## Voice
- **Crafted, not corporate.** Write like a maker with sawdust on their jeans.
- **Confident about the craft, humble about the hustle.** No hype, no jargon.
- **Sensory.** Talk about grain, burn marks, the way brass patinas.
- **Short sentences. Deliberate pauses.** Like a good cut.

**Do say:** "Hand-finished." "Shop-made." "One of twelve." "Cut on the Shapeoko."
**Don't say:** "Luxury." "Premium quality." "Revolutionary." "Disruptive."

## Brand Pillars
1. **Material honesty** — show the grain, the toolpath, the rivet.
2. **Editioned, not mass.** Limited runs, numbered pieces, signed backs.
3. **Studio-to-door.** You order it, we cut it, we box it, we ship it.
4. **The garage is the gallery.** Process is part of the product.
