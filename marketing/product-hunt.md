# Ironlace Studio — Product Hunt Launch

**Tagline:** Precision cut. Artfully made.

**Short pitch (60 chars):**
Heirloom CNC work from a one-person garage shop. Shipped.

**Extended pitch (260 chars):**
Ironlace Studio is a CNC garage shop turning raw steel, walnut, and aluminum into editioned objects — signage, furniture inlays, wall art, custom hardware. Cut on the Shapeoko. Hand-finished. Numbered. Signed on the back. Order from the shop floor, we ship to your door.

## First comment (maker)

Hey Hunters — I'm the one running the saw.

I built Ironlace Studio because I wanted somewhere the process was part of the product. You see the toolpath. You see the grain. You see the burn mark where the end mill lingered a half-second too long, and we kept it anyway.

Everything on the site is shop-made, in limited runs. If the listing says "One of twelve," that's what you're getting — one of twelve, signed on the back, boxed and shipped from the garage.

What's live today:
- Full catalog — steel signage, walnut inlays, aluminum wall pieces, custom hardware
- Direct Stripe checkout with live shipping rates
- Custom commission intake — send me a vector, I'll send you a quote
- Order tracking from cut to crate
- A shop journal so you can see what's on the table this week

If you've ever wanted a piece that wasn't stamped out by the thousand, this is for you. Happy to answer anything about the work, the machine, or the mistakes I've learned from.

— Signed, the guy with sawdust on his jeans.
